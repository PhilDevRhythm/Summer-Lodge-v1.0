Winter Season

The more he sharpened his mind, the more the absurdities of the world came into stark relief. And so Okina renounced it all, and rose to a higher plane. There was only himself. His katana. And its mastery. Before long, Okina became a demon of a swordsman. Ancestral Follower Shaman is an Enemy in Elden Ring. They can be heard singing throughout the Ancestral Woods in Siofra River. Their singing produces a glowing blue aura that grants a buff to nearby Ancestral Follower allies. When they are confronted alone they will stop singing and attack the player with an Ancestral Infant's Head and melee with their hand.
